Rules about profile photos:

  1. Must show your face

    The photo must show your actual face.

  2. Face must be prominent

    Only your head and shoulders may be visible. The photo should not be zoomed out.

  3. Just you

    The photo may show you, and only you. No other people should be visible.

  4. Must be appropriate

    The photo must have a modest, dignified and wholesome quality.
